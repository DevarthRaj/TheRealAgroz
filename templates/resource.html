BLOGS

AI for Crop and Resource Management: AI models are enhancing farming efficiency by predicting soil and water conditions, helping farmers optimize resource use and reduce environmental impact.

Precision Robots for Farming: Farm robots, like Carbon Robotics' LaserWeeder, use AI to target weeds without chemicals, improving crop care and sustainability.

Predictive Soil Management: Research institutions are developing predictive models to understand soil properties and their effects on water availability, improving long-term crop planning.

Drone Technology for Carbon Sequestration: Drones are being used to measure and verify carbon sequestration on farms, enabling easier participation in carbon credit markets.

Autonomous Farm Equipment: AGCO's autonomous tractors, like Fendt Xaver robots, offer precise planting and improved farm productivity using AI and real-time data.
